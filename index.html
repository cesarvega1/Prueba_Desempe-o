About the Band

Aerosmith is a legendary American rock band formed in Boston in 1970, with Steven Tyler, Joe Perry, Tom Hamilton, Joey Kramer, and Brad Whitford. Known as "The Bad Boys from Boston," they blended blues-based hard rock with pop, metal, and R&B, selling over 150 million albums worldwide. Their hits include "Dream On," "Walk Down This Way," and "Don't Wanna Miss a Thing."
